<br />
# Controls
Player 1:<br />
-  'wasd keys' to move<br />
-  'enter' or 't' to pause<br />
-  'escape' or 'backspace' to exit<br />

Player 2:<br />
-  'arrow keys' to move<br />
-  'enter' or 't' to pause<br />
-  'escape' or 'backspace' to exit<br />
